Our Services

Take the guesswork out of accounting information systems.

Education

We can train your staff on the use of accounting and ERP software, or on specific accounting topics such as inventory management. We also discuss the rationale and importance of general accounting principles from different team role perspectives. Large group, small group, and individual mentoring are all available.

Design

We can research and recommend an accounting software solution appropriate for your company, or if needed, custom-design an accounting system based on your organizational requirements. We can also provide recommendations on specific facets of a proposed system, such as security/usability tradeoffs.

Deployment

The transition to a new accounting system is a critical and sensitive business phase. We offer staggered rollout planning and implementation as well as data backup and reconciliation services. We also suggest new control procedures and tweaks to accommodate employee feedback and unexpected workflow disruptions.

Management

Punjab Financial can meet all of your small business or non-profit bookkeeping needs. We provide financial services remotely or on-site depending on your budget. Although we do not provide assurance services, we do offer audit preparation and coordination services to accommodate external audits.

Tax Planning

The tax environment is in constant flux and requires thorough analysis and impact planning. Our consulting catalog covers the entire scope of tax services, from income tax projections to cash flow planning to offers in compromise. We also prepare partnership, corporate, non-profit, and amended return tax forms.

Special Projects

If you need temporary or one-off assistance, Punjab Financial can provide you with the accounting expertise you require. Examples include transferring data from a paper-based system to a digital database system or providing extra staffing to cope with unexpected growth in your clientele.

About Us

What's most important to us is building lasting relationships.

We are a team of professional accountants specializing in small businesses, startups, and non-profit organizations. Tejdev Sandhu, our managing partner and a Massachusetts-licensed CPA, was born in a working-class immigrant family that struggled to establish new businesses in the United States beginning in the 1970s. Through these growing pains, Tejdev recognized there was a great need for business education amongst the large but traditionally underserved immigrant and non-English speaking communities. More broadly, he believed that entrepreneurs had no shortage of innovative ideas and willpower to succeed, but they lacked the business know-how to turn these concepts into reality.

Tejdev leveraged his experience as a former member of IBM's global business consulting team to begin consulting directly with small business owners. He founded Punjab Financial, LLC so that his mission of spreading business know-how to new entrepreneurs could be expanded. We now have three partners of diverse backgrounds and skill sets serving multiple localities in the Northern Virginia area. Whatever your organization's current status and goals, Punjab Financial has the expertise and experience to accelerate your performance.
